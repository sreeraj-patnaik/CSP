How we did it

Not a report written from a desk.

Under the guidance of {{ mentor_name }} at {{ college_name }}, the team visited homes, community halls, and spoke directly with farmers, homemakers, daily-wage workers, and students about their digital lives.

The result is a ground-level picture of internet access, risk exposure, and awareness that no remote survey could capture.
